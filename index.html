VOICE ACTRESS SIGNED · WEISS SCHWARZ SPECIALS

Signed SP Cards

Personal artifacts, not just cards. Each SP is hand-signed by the voice actress who voiced that character — pulled at ultra-rare rates. When a VA retires, these become irreplaceable cultural relics.

✍️ What Makes SP Cards Different?

Weiss Schwarz SP cards are hand-signed by the voice actress who voices that character. Not printed. Not stamped. Hand-signed, one at a time, at approximately 1-in-12-20 boxes. Each signature is unique. When a VA retires or passes, their signed cards become permanently finite artifacts that can never be recreated.

✍️
Real Signatures
Hand-signed by the actual VA — unique pen pressure and character on every card.
💎
Ultra-Rare Pull Rate
1 per 12–20 boxes. Some SP are 1-of-a-kind in PSA/BGS populations worldwide.
📈
Appreciates Like Fine Art
Signed items historically outperform unsigned. Same dynamic applies in anime TCG.
🎌
Cultural Time Capsules
A VA's signature at peak career. These are moments frozen in cardboard.
